Ingredients:
 - 8 ounces unsalted butter
 - 2 tablespoons ice
 - 10 ounces all-purpose flour
 - 3/4 teaspoon backing soda
 - 2 teaspoons kosher salt
 - 5 ounces granulated sugar
 - 2 large eggs
 - 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
 - 5 ounces dark brown sugar
 - 4 ounces 54% cocoa chocolate
 - 4 ounces 71% cocoa chocolate
 - Coarse sea salt for garnish

Directions:
 1. Brown butter, transfer to medium bowl and whisk in ice, transfer to
 refrigerator and allow to cool whisking occasionally
 2. Whisk together flour, baking soda, salt in larg bowl.
 3. Mix sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract in stamd mixer with whisk on medium
 high until thick ribbons fall when lifted.
 4. add brown sugar and cooled butter mix on medium with paddle for 15 seconds
 until incorperated.
 5. Add flour mixture and mix on low until barely combined.
 6. Add choclate and mix on low until dough comes together.
 7. Transfer to an airtight container and refrigerate at least overnight and up
 to 3 days.
 8. When ready to bake preheat oven to 325 and cook 1.5 ounce balls for ~12
 minutes until botttoms are well carmelized and tops are dry, flipping tray
 around at the mid way point.
